The Revolution of Dignity
(November 21, 2013 - February 2014)

Overview

The Revolution of Dignity — mass protests in Ukraine sparked by the government's rejection of European integration and the crackdown on students, which culminated in Viktor Yanukovych's flight from the country. Key stages included the peaceful Euromaidan, the beatings on November 30, the “March of Millions,” the adoption of “dictatorial laws,” the bloody clashes of February 18-20, and the dictator's flight.
